Clarkson Village is the oldest community in the city of Mississauga, Ontario. Located in the south west corner of Mississauga, the Clarkson Village is home to numerous fine dining establishments, unique stores and ample parking to allow you to explore this distinct community with all the amenities.

Running along Lakeshore Road West you’ll find the small-town ambience of Clarkson Village with its cafés, shops, lakeside parks, pubs and wine bars. Directly across the street, is the convenience of a 24-hour Metro Superstore, Shopper’s Drug Mart, Canadian Tire, Starbucks, and Whole Foods is just 5 minutes away.

Developed as a suburb of Toronto, Mississauga is located near the shores of Lake Ontario, in the Regional Municipality of Peel. The city has a population of 713,443 as of the Canada 2011 Census, and is the 37th largest suburb in the world by population.
